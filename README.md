# My Favorite Series: The Summer I Turned Pretty

## Overview
**The Summer I Turned Pretty** is a romantic drama series based on the novel trilogy by *Jenny Han*. The show premiered on **Amazon Prime Video in June 2022** and has captured hearts with its nostalgic summer vibes and coming-of-age story.

## Plot Summary
